Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: October 21, 2025

At Grand Aura, we understand that plans can change. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines our terms for booking modifications, cancellations, and refunds. Please read this policy carefully before making a reservation.

⚠️ Important Notice

All deposits are non-refundable. However, depending on when you cancel, you may be eligible for a partial refund of payments beyond the deposit.

1. Deposit Policy

To secure your wedding venue reservation, a deposit of 25% of the total booking cost is required. This deposit:

  • Is non-refundable under all circumstances
  • Secures your chosen date and venue
  • Covers administrative and coordination costs
  • Is applied toward your total booking cost

2. Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel your reservation, the amount refunded depends on when you notify us:

Cancellation Timeline Refund Amount
More than 6 months before event 75% refund (deposit forfeited)
3-6 months before event 50% refund (deposit forfeited)
1-3 months before event 25% refund (deposit forfeited)
Less than 1 month before event No refund

2.1 How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation:

  1. Contact us via email at bookings@grandaura.com or phone at +94 11 234 5678
  2. Provide your booking confirmation number
  3. Submit cancellation request in writing
  4. Receive cancellation confirmation within 3 business days

Note: Cancellations are effective from the date we receive your written notice, not from the date you initiate contact.

3. Modification Policy

3.1 Date Changes

If you need to change your event date:

  • More than 6 months before: Free date change (subject to availability)
  • 3-6 months before: Rs. 50,000 modification fee
  • 1-3 months before: Rs. 100,000 modification fee
  • Less than 1 month: Date changes not permitted

3.2 Guest Count Changes

  • Increases: Permitted up to 2 weeks before event (venue capacity permitting)
  • Decreases: Final guest count must be confirmed 2 weeks before event. No refunds for lower attendance than confirmed count

3.3 Venue Changes

Changing to a different venue at Grand Aura:

  • Subject to availability
  • Price difference will be adjusted accordingly
  • Rs. 25,000 administrative fee applies
  • Not permitted within 1 month of event date

4. Refund Processing

4.1 Timeline

Once your cancellation is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 10-15 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Bank processing times may vary (allow 7-10 additional business days)

4.2 Refund Method

  • Credit/debit card payments: Refunded to original card
  • Bank transfers: Refunded to original bank account
  • Third-party payment services: Subject to their refund policies

5. Force Majeure and Extraordinary Circumstances

5.1 Definition

Force majeure events include circumstances beyond our reasonable control:

  • Natural disasters (earthquakes, floods, tsunamis)
  • Government-mandated closures or restrictions
  • Pandemics or public health emergencies
  • Acts of terrorism or war
  • Major infrastructure failures

5.2 Our Response

In the event of force majeure:

  • We will notify you as soon as possible
  • We will work with you to reschedule your event at no additional charge
  • If rescheduling is not possible, we will provide a full refund including the deposit
  • We are not liable for any consequential damages or expenses

5.3 Customer-Initiated Cancellations Due to Force Majeure

If you need to cancel due to force majeure on your end (e.g., illness, family emergency):

  • Standard cancellation policy applies
  • We recommend purchasing event insurance for such circumstances
  • We will do our best to work with you on rescheduling options

6. No-Show Policy

If you do not arrive for your event and have not cancelled:

  • The full booking amount is forfeited
  • No refund will be provided
  • The venue and services are still reserved and cannot be offered to others

7. Partial Service Cancellations

If you wish to cancel specific add-on services (catering upgrades, decorations, etc.):

  • More than 30 days before: Full refund for the service
  • 15-30 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 15 days: No refund

8. Booking Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing event or wedding insurance to protect your investment. Event insurance can cover:

  • Cancellation or postponement costs
  • Illness or injury
  • Venue or vendor issues
  • Extreme weather
  • Military deployment

9. Disputes and Exceptions

If you believe exceptional circumstances warrant special consideration:

  1. Contact our management team at management@grandaura.com
  2. Provide detailed explanation and supporting documentation
  3. We will review on a case-by-case basis
  4. Final decisions rest with Grand Aura management
